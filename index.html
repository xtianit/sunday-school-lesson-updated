INTRODUCTION

The fact that the status of a man changes with the level of position he attains is true as it is pictured from our text and in 2 Cor. 5:17 when you were a child, you thought like one but when you become an adult, you reason as one - 1 Cor. 13:11.

Anything short of these will arouse the interest of people to ascertain your sanity. If you are a new creature then bring forth fruit as a proof - Matt 3:8.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

Aim: To draw the attention of Christians to the need of change of character.

Objective: That the child of God may be known by deed and not by words.

TEXT REVIEW/LESSON

God said that you are a new creature and being who you are, you are required to live in the new style of life and not the former (Gal 5:25).

In our last study we learned about long suffering but today we will x-ray forbearance.

BOWELS OF FORBEARANCE

Another word for forbearance is tolerance. It means to accept an unpleasant person without complaining or to allow somebody to do something that you do not like.

It also means to overlook or to absorb pain caused by someone as though it has no effect. God tolerates us so much and he wants us to have the same attributes on your spirit man which should manifest since you are his image.

Hab. 1:13 says God can't look at evil yet for our sins Jesus died while we were still sinners - Rom 5:8 and He keps absorbing our excesses until we surrender to HIm - Rev. 3:20

The Necessity for Forbearance

  1. It is a Christian requirement - Eph. 4:1-2, 2 Cor 12:6
  2. It gives room for repentance - Rom 2:4
  3. It keeps relationship - Prov 17:9
  4. It reveals a person - Matt 7:20, Titus 2:7-8, 2 Pet 3:1-2
  5. It avoids crisis - Prov. 15:18, 16:32, 20:3

CONCLUSION

For you that say "I don't tolerate nonsense!", God is saying that you may not tolerate the nonsense but tolerate the person. If God had'nt tolerated you, you would be in the fire of hell BY now - Rom 3:25. Tolerate one another for there is only one judge who is in heaven before whom we all stand.
